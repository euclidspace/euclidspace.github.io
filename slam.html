Euclid Insight

Environment Perception System.

Overview :

The most important step for any system to interact with the world autonomously is understanding the environment, i.e it's Perception of the Real World. Euclid has developed accurate perception systems that can be integrated into almost any system starting from a Robotic Arm, Drones, etc. to an Autonomous Car, where our system provides robots and other autonomous systems 3D pointcloud data of the world around them just by through an ordinary camera.

Current robots and an autonomous systems are obtaining 3 Dimentional data of the environment only with sensors like Radars, LiDARs, which cost a huge amount even to integrate one such sensor. But with the help of latest advances in Computer Vision and Deep Learning and the open Research Culture that has took over the world, Euclid has been able to produce the cheapest system that uses cameras to produce these dense 3D poindcloud and help autonomy in ways that were not possible before.
